Shut Up, Make Love

"Shut Up, Make Love" is a song by American rock\ref{Rock_music} band Poison\ref{Poison_(American_band)}; released in 2000, it was the first single presented to radio stations from their 2000\ref{2000_in_music} "Crack a Smile...and More!\ref{Crack_a_Smile}" album. The song also appears on the second promo single "Be the One\ref{Be_the_One_(Poison_song)}" as a B-side. "Crack a Smile" is Poison's fifth studio album, released on March 14, 2000, and charting at #131 on The Billboard 200\ref{The_Billboard_200}. The song was the first Poison single with lead guitarist Blues Saraceno\ref{Blues_Saraceno}.
