Freakonomics (film)

Freakonomics: The Movie is a 2010 American documentary film\ref{documentary_film} based on the 2005 book "Freakonomics\ref{Freakonomics}: A Rogue Economist Explores the Hidden Side of Everything" by economist Steven D. Levitt\ref{Steven_D._Levitt} and writer Stephen J. Dubner\ref{Stephen_J._Dubner}. The film premiered at the Tribeca Film Festival\ref{Tribeca_Film_Festival} in April 2010 with a theatrical release later in the year. The film received a lukewarm reception and a 65% fresh rating at Rottentomatoes.
