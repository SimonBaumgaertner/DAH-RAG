Brown State Fishing Lake

Brown State Fishing Lake (sometimes also known as Brown State Fishing Lake And Wildlife Area) is a protected area\ref{protected_area} in Brown County\ref{Brown_County,_Kansas}, Kansas\ref{Kansas} in the United States\ref{United_States}. The lake is 62 acres (0.25 km²) in area and up to 13 feet (4 m) deep. The area was formerly known as Brown County State Park, and is 8 miles (13 km) east of Hiawatha, Kansas\ref{Hiawatha,_Kansas}.
