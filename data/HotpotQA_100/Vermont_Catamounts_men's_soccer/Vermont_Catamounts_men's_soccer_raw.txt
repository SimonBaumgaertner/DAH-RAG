Vermont Catamounts men's soccer

The Vermont Catamounts men's soccer team represents the University of Vermont\ref{University_of_Vermont} in all NCAA Division I\ref{NCAA_Division_I} men's college soccer\ref{college_soccer} competitions. The team competes in the America East Conference\ref{America_East_Conference}.

The soccer team currently practices at the Virtue Field adjacent to the Centennial Field\ref{Centennial_Field} on campus.
