Sachin Warrier

Sachin Warrier is a playback singer and composer in the Malayalam cinema industry\ref{Malayalam_cinema} from Kerala\ref{Kerala}. He became notable with the song "Muthuchippi Poloru\ref{Muthuchippi_Poloru}" from the film Thattathin Marayathu\ref{Thattathin_Marayathu}. He made his debut with the movie Malarvaadi Arts Club\ref{Malarvaadi_Arts_Club}. He was working as a software engineer in Tata Consultancy Services\ref{Tata_Consultancy_Services} in Kochi\ref{Kochi}. Later he resigned from the job to concentrate more on music. His latest work is as a composer for the movie Aanandam\ref{Aanandam}.

Sachin was born in Calicut\ref{Kozhikode}, and grew up in Kottakkal\ref{Kottakkal}, in Malappuram\ref{Malappuram_district} district. He finished his engineering at FISAT, Angamaly, Kerala\ref{Federal_Institute_of_Science_and_Technology}.

He made his debut in playback singing while he was in college, in the year 2010, for the movie Malarvaadi Arts Club\ref{Malarvaadi_Arts_Club}. He then joined Tata Consultancy Services\ref{Tata_Consultancy_Services} and worked for three years, during which period he sang for movies including Thattathin Marayathu\ref{Thattathin_Marayathu}, Neram\ref{Neram}, Left Right Left\ref{Left_Right_Left_(film)}, Thira\ref{Thira_(film)}, Rasputin\ref{Rasputin_(2015_film)}, Philips and the Monkey Pen\ref{Philips_and_the_Monkey_Pen} and Bangalore Days\ref{Bangalore_Days}. In late 2014, he left his IT job to focus more on music. He also started composing for ads and making music independently around this period. His debut single as an independent musician, called 'Naam' (meaning 'us', in Malayalam) was released in 2015, and premiered in Pepsi MTV Indies channel.

His first movie as a composer was Aanandam\ref{Aanandam}, released in 2016.
