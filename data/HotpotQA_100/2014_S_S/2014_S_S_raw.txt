2014 S/S

2014 S/S is the debut album of South Korea\ref{South_Korea}n group\ref{musical_ensemble} WINNER\ref{Winner_(band)}. It was released on August 12, 2014 by the group's record label, YG Entertainment\ref{YG_Entertainment}. The members were credited for writing the lyrics and composing the majority of the album's songs.

The title track "Empty" became a hit in South Korea, topping the Gaon Chart\ref{Gaon_Chart} and Billboard's K-Pop Hot 100 chart. Internationally, "2014 S/S" took the top spot in Billboard\ref{Billboard_(magazine)}'s World Album Chart.

The members produced the majority of the music for this album themselves, with the help of other producers such as Choice 37, Airplay, and others.
