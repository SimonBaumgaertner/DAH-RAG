Arena of Khazan

Arena of Khazan is a 1979 role-playing game adventure for "Tunnels & Trolls\ref{Tunnels_&amp;_Trolls}" published by Flying Buffalo\ref{Flying_Buffalo}.

"Arena of Khazan" is a scenario of short and brutal arena combat in the City of Death.

Lorin Rivers reviewed "Arena of Khazan" in "The Space Gamer\ref{The_Space_Gamer}" No. 28. Rivers commented that "This adventure can be fun, but not recommended for highly-valued characters."
