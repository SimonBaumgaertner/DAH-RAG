Seven Brief Lessons on Physics

Seven Brief Lessons on Physics (Italian\ref{Italian_language}: "" ) is a short book by the Italian physicist Carlo Rovelli\ref{Carlo_Rovelli}. Originally published in Italian\ref{Italian_language} in 2014, the book has been translated into 41 languages. It is an Italian bestseller, with more than 300,000 copies sold in Rovelli's home country.

The book condenses the revelations of post-Newtonian physics\ref{modern_physics} – from Einstein's theory of relativity\ref{theory_of_relativity} to quantum mechanics\ref{quantum_mechanics} – into seven brief, accessible lessons.
